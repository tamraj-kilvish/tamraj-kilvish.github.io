India's first social payment app

  • Pay by creating a unique url & both you & receiver can track payments against it. Useful for recurring payments.
  • Kilvish redirects to your favorite UPI app & consolidates all UPI expenses at one place.
  • Tag each expense (or the url) as food, travel, grocery. Create your own tags.
  • Track group expenses against a url by sharing it with everyone involved.
Try Kilvish

Track/collect payment against url

  • Create a url & pay against it. Send url to the payer if you look to collect payment.
  • Both the payer & payee can track payment against the url including past payments.
  • Great for recurring shared household expense like paying for newspaper. Keep paying every month against the same url to keep track of all past payments.

Easily track recurring payment to vendors

  • Create url for specific vendor like newspaper delivery person.
  • Share the url with other household members. Any member can pay against the url. Past payments on the url will be visible to all members.
  • Newspaper guy can also track all past payments post signing up in the app

Shared expense tracking through tags

  • Create own tags like newspaper, travel, car etc. Tag url or individual expense. Get consolidated monthly expense against each tag.
  • Create group tags like one with your flatmates. Attaching a group tag to an expense automatically shares it with same set of people. Filter expenses by group tag & see consolidated payment per person.
  • Create a url/tag for a trip & everyone can pay against it. See consolidated payments & settle at the end. Bye bye shared expense apps.

Crowdsourcing payments

  • Looking to book football ground & wanting to collect payment from everyone ? Do so by creating url & everyone involved can see who paid & how much.
  • Looking to run a crowd funding activity for a cause. Create a public url which hides confidential information of payers but everyone can see list of payers against it & total amount contributed.

Its Free .. 99%

  • It will not cost at all, ever, for paying out. Period. You can use Kilvish to keep your payments well managed. You can even import outside Kilvish payments for better accounting purpose. Consider it Tally for masses but free :-).
  • If you use Kilvish to collect payment, you may need to pay if you cross a certain threshold of total amount or number of transactions. We are yet to decide. But till then, it is free.
